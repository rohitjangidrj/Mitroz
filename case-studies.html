    BVG Group

    1

    Client

    A conglomerate with business interests in multiple industry sectors.

    2

    Need/Challenge

    Inability to track business performance in real time owing to lack of aggregated data. Hence the management couldn’t monitor the movement of products, increasing the risk of theft or loss of items in the value chain. Also, real time order management and inventory management were uphill tasks.

    3

    Mitroz Solution

    After studying the business workflows, Mitroz created a mobile app and cloud-based solution that addressed all the above issues satisfactorily. This android app has an interface for the sales team, distributors and dealers; the robust solution is characterized by modules such as product catalogue, price list, product plans, order booking and payment collection, among others.

    4

    Outcome

    Digitalization of key tasks and processes streamlined the entire operations landscape, brining clarity and visibility at all touchpoints. This enabled greater growth for retailers and distributors while resulting in higher productivity and profitability for the company.
